Custom vim keybindings:
  Insert mode:
    jj to escape insert mode
    Ctrl V to paste
    Tab + Shift tab to ident
  Normal mode:
    Tab + Shift tab to ident
    Ctrl + h,j,k,l to swap splits if you have splits
    Ctrl + arrowkeys to increase current split size
  Visual mode:
    Tab + Shift tab to ident

LSP:
  Normal mode:
    gd to go to definition
    gD to go to declaration (header files in cpp)
    gr to go to all references
    gi to go to implementation
    K to hover
